Ching-Chih Amber Tsao

 

I'm a first-year master's student in Information Systems - Connective Media at Cornell University (Cornell Tech). I'm currently working on studying social discomfort in shared rides in the Future Automation Research Laboratory led by Dr. Wendy Ju. Previously, I worked as an undergraduate research assistant in the Human-Automation Interaction Lab at National Chengchi University, supervised by Dr. Shih-Yi Chien. My research interests focus on Human-Automation Decision Making, with particular attention to Human-Robot Collaboration (HRC) and Cognitive Enhancement. My previous research projects utilized EEG analysis to assess user behavior and decision-making strategies during HRC.
